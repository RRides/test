Why a cooperative?

The mission of RightRides is to be an true alternative to the independent contractor model dominating the TNC market and provide members with democratic control and equity.

How will RightRides compete?

Innovatively! In contrast to the traditional tech business model creating a minimum viable product capable of generating viral growth. RightRides will first focus on building membership as the product is a commodity clone and is differentiated only by the ownership structure.
